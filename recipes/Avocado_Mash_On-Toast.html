Avocado Mash On Toast

How to make Avocado Mash On Toast

The velvety nuttiness of avocado punctuated by the peppery crunch of spring onions

the sharp tang of lime juice and the refreshing earthiness of coriander leaves

spread thickly on a toasted bread paired with soothing Earl Grey is my idea of a heavenly breakfast on the go.

Ingredients of Avocado Mash On Toast

  • 2 Avocado
  • 4 Slices of bread (preferably sour dough)
  • 2 Spring onions, chopped
  • 1 Lime juice
  • 1 tbsp Coriander , chopped
  • 1 tsp Green chilli, chopped
  • Optional:
  • 2 tsp Olive oil
  • to taste Black salt

How to Make Avocado Mash On Toast

  1. Lightly toast the slices of bread and keep aside to cool down.
  2. Peel the avocado and scoop out the flesh in a bowl.
  3. Add spring onions, lime juice, coriander, green chilli, olive oil and black salt. Mix well.
  4. Spread it evenly on the toasted bread and enjoy.
